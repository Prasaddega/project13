DAVID WARNER

David Andrew Warner (born 27 October 1986) is an Australian international cricketer and a former captain of the Australian national team in limited overs format and a former Test vice-captain. A left-handed opening batsman, Warner is the first Australian cricketer in 132 years to be selected for a national team in any format without experience in first-class cricket. He is widely considered as one of the greatest batsman of his era. He plays for New South Wales and played for the Sydney Thunder in domestic cricket.
